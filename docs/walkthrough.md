# Walkthrough



## What the Help Site Is For

Use the Help Site to:

- Learn how the inventory system works
- Follow step-by-step walkthroughs
- Troubleshoot common issues
- Understand features and terminology
- Find answers without contacting support

The Help Site is available 24/7 and is the fastest way to get help.

---

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Help Site

You can open the Help Site by:

- Clicking **Ctrl + ?** inside any module of the inventory system
- Visiting the Help Site URL directly **https://help.ftsware.com/index.html**

No login is required unless you are viewing role-specific content.

## When to Contact Support

Contact support if:
- The Help Site does not resolve your issue
- You experience system errors
- You need account or permission changes

---

## How to Contact Support

- Reach out to your local Account Manager
- Reach out to your local Vending Team

Include screenshots or error messages for faster assistance.

---

## Tips for Using the Help Site Effectively

- Bookmark frequently used articles
- Search before contacting your local support team
- Follow walkthroughs step by step
