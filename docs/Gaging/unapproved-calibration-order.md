# Unapproved Calibration Orders

Unapproved calibration orders allow users to **prepare and organize gage calibration requests** before officially submitting them. This workflow lets multiple supervisors contribute gages to a single order while preventing calibration suppliers from receiving incomplete requests.

**Also known as:** *Gage Calibration Orders*, *Unapproved Calibration Orders*

---

## How to Create an Unapproved Calibration Order

### 1. Access the Gaging Module

1. Log in to the system.
2. From your dashboard, click the **Gaging** module.
3. Select **Calibration Orders** to open the Open Calibration Orders page.

---

### 2. Search for Gages

Use one or more of the following search criteria:

- **Station**
- **Calibrator** assigned to the gage
- **Percent of calibration life remaining**
- **Expiration date**

Once the desired criteria are entered, click **Next** to continue.

---

### 2. Create a New Calibration Order

1. On the Open Calibration Orders page, click **Create New Calibration Order**.
2. The gage search screen will appear.

---

### 3. Search for Gages

Use one or more of the following search criteria:

- **Station**
- **Calibrator** assigned to the gage
- **Percent of calibration life remaining**
- **Expiration date**

Once the desired criteria are entered, click **Next** to continue.

---

### 4. Build the Calibration Order

- A new calibration order is created for the selected supplier.
- Add addtional gages to the order by:
  - Clicking **Add New Line** to manually add gages.

To view additional details for a gage, click the **( + )** icon under the line item.

---

### 5. Mark the Order as Unapproved

To keep the order unapproved:

1. Uncheck the **Approved** checkbox.
2. Click **Save**.

When an order is unapproved:
- The **Approved** field may appear in red, or
- The approval indicator may be blank

Both indicate the order is not approved.

---

### 6. Email Notifications

After saving, you may be prompted to send the order:

- **Do not send the order to the calibration supplier** while it is unapproved.
- You may send it to another **registered supervisor** so they can review or add gages to the order.

---

### 7. Verify Order Status

- The unapproved calibration order will appear in **Open Calibration Orders**.
- It will be displayed **in red**, indicating that approval is still required.

---

## Important Notes

- Unapproved calibration orders are intended for **internal coordination only**.
- Suppliers should receive **approved calibration orders only**.
- The workflow is similar to issuing or purchasing processes that follow a staged approval model.
