# Physical a Gage in Location

Phyiscal a gage allows it to become **active and usable in the FTS system**. Assign a gage to a cabinet location and updates the on‑hand quantity so it can be tracked and issued correctly.

**Also known as:** *Physical Gage In*, *Assign Gage to Location*

---

## How to Physical a Gage

You can assign a gage location from either the **Cabinets Module** or the **Kiosk**.

---

## Option A: Physical a Gage from the Cabinets Module

### 1. Open the Cabinet Module

1. Log in to the system.
2. From your dashboard, click the **Cabinet Module**.
3. Open the **Cabinet List**.

---


### 2. Select a Cabinet

1. In the Cabinet List, click the cabinet where the gage will be installed.
2. Choose the appropriate drawer and bin location.

---

### 3. Find the Correct Location

You can locate a bin using several methods:

- **Drawer Select**
  - Navigate directly to a specific drawer and view its open bins.
- **Scroll Through Bin List**
  - Scroll through available bin locations and click **Select**.
- **Search**
  - Enter the **gage description** or **item ID** in the search box to narrow results.

Select the bin location where the gage will be physically placed.

---

### 4. Physical Gage Quantity (Physical In)

Once the gage has been received from the supplier or is physically on hand:

1. Click directly on the number in the **On Hand QTY** field.
2. Enter the quantity being installed.
3. Click **OK** to confirm.

---

### 5. Confirm Serialized Gage

If the gage is serialized:

- A confirmation screen appears asking if this specific serialized gage should be physical in the selected bin.
- Confirm your selection to proceed.

---

## Completion

- The **new quantity on hand** will now appear.
- The gage is officially installed, active, and ready for use in the system.

---

## Option B: Physical a Gage from the Kiosk



### 1. Log In

1. Sign in using your assigned credentials.
2. Enter your information and click **Submit**.

After logging in, you will see the **front page of your site**, displaying items at your station.

---

### 2. Open Physical Inventory

1. From the site front page, click the **Physical** link

This opens the Physical Inventory list.

---

### 4. Select an Item

- Click **anywhere on the item’s row** to select it  
- There is **no separate “Select” link**—the entire row is clickable

---

### 5. Confirm Serialized Gage

If the gage is serialized:

- A confirmation screen appears asking if this specific serialized gage should be physical in the selected bin.
- Confirm your selection to proceed.

---

### 6. Return to Home

- Use the **Back** link at the top of the page to return to the home screen after completing the adjustment.
