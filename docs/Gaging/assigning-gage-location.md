# Assign Gage Location

Assigning a gage location allows users to **install, track, and manage individual gages** within cabinets and bins. This ensures each gage is associated with a specific location and serial number for accurate tracking.

**Also known as:** *Loading a Gage*, *Installing a Gage*, *Assign Gage to Bin*

---

## How to Assign a Gage Location

You can assign a gage location from either the **Cabinets Module** or the **Items Module**.

---

## Option A: Assign a Gage from the Cabinets Module

### 1. Open Cabinets

1. Log in to the **Cloud Dashboard**.
2. Click the **Cabinets** module.

---

### 2. Select Cabinet and Bin Location

1. Select the cabinet where the gage will be installed.
2. Choose a bin location by:
   - Scrolling through the list, or
   - Using **Drawer Select** to jump to a specific drawer.

---

### 3. Add an Item to the Bin

1. From the bin location, click **Select** in the item section.
2. Locate the item by:
   - Scrolling through the list, or
   - Entering the **item code** in the search box.
3. Click **Select** to load the item into the bin.

If the item does not exist:
- Click the **plus (+)** icon, or
- Click **Create Item**  

---

### 4. Assign a Specific Gage (Serial Number)

After the item is loaded, the location is assigned only to the **item code**.

To track a specific gage:

1. Click **Select** under **Gage (Serial Number)**.
2. Choose the correct gage from the list.
3. Verify the serial number appears in the location details.

---

### 5. Update Location

- Click **Update Location** to lock in the assignment.
- The gage is now permanently associated with that bin.

---

## Option B: Assign a Gage from the Items Module

### 1. Open Item Locations

1. Navigate to the **Items Module**.
2. Click the **Location(s)** tab.
3. Click the **here** link to view cabinet options.

---

### 2. Select Cabinet and Bin

1. Select the cabinet.
2. Choose the bin location.
3. Click **Select** to assign the item to that location.

---

### 3. Assign Gage Serial Number

1. Click **Select** under **Gage (Serial Number)**.
2. Choose the correct gage.
3. Confirm the serial number is displayed.

---

### 4. Finalize Assignment

- Click **Update Location** to save changes.
- The gage is now installed and tracked at that location.

---

## Location Format Example

- **Cabinet:** 01  
- **Column:** A  
- **Row:** 01  

Displayed as **01-A-01**

---

## Important Notes

- Assigning an item does **not** automatically assign a gage.
- A **serial number must be selected** to track an individual gage.
- Always click **Update Location** to finalize changes.
