# Pick Gage for Calibration

Picking a gage allows users to **remove a gage from active inventory** so it can be sent out for calibration. This process updates the gage’s status and notifies other users that the gage is unavailable while it is off‑site.

**Also known as:** *Pick Gage*, *Remove Gage for Calibration*

---

## How to Pick a Gage

### 1. Open the Gaging Module

1. Log in to the system.
2. From your **Dashboard**, click **Gaging**.
3. Select **Calibration Orders** to view open orders.

---

### 2. Open a Calibration Order

1. In the **Open Calibration Orders** list, locate your order.
2. Click the **Order Number** to open the order details.

---

### 3. Pick the Gage

1. In the **Status** section, locate the **Not Picked** link.
2. Click **Not Picked** to begin the removal process.
3. When prompted, confirm that you want to change the gage status to **Picked**.

---

## Gage Status Change

After the gage is picked:

- **Not Picked** is replaced with **Out For Calibration**
- The gage is removed from available inventory
- Other users are notified that the gage is unavailable

---

## Remove and Ship the Gage

1. Go to the kiosk or machine location.
2. Physically remove the gage.
3. Prepare the gage for shipment to the calibration provider.

> The gage is already removed from the system’s picking process, so no additional checkout steps are required.

---

## Important Notes

- Picking the gage is required before physical removal.
- Once marked **Out For Calibration**, the gage cannot be issued or used.
- Verify the correct gage before confirming the pick.
