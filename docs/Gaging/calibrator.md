# Creating a Calibrator

Creating a **Calibrator** allows you to set up a supplier that manages **calibration orders and calibration-related information**.  
A calibrator functions similarly to a supplier and is used to track, send, and manage calibration orders for your equipment.

**Also known as:** *Calibration Supplier*, *Gaging Supplier*

---

## What Is a Calibrator?

A Calibrator is a supplier record used specifically for handling calibration services.  
It stores contact details, delivery instructions, purchase order defaults, and communication settings required to process calibration orders efficiently.

---

## How to Create a Calibrator

### 1. Access the Suppliers Module

1. Log in to the system.
2. From the **Dashboard**, click the **Suppliers** module.

You will be taken to the **Supplier List** page, which displays all suppliers currently in your database.

---

### 2. Create a New Supplier

1. On the Supplier List page, click **Create New Supplier**.
2. The Create Supplier form will open.

This form is used for both standard suppliers and calibrators.

---

### 3. Enter Required Information

The following fields are **required** to create a calibrator:

- **Supplier Name**  
  The company or internal group performing the calibration  
  *(Example: “Internal Calibration”)*

- **Email Contact**  
  The primary email address used to send calibration orders and notifications  
  (Multiple email addresses may be included)

- **Supplier Address**  
  A **ZIP code is required**  
  *(Best practice is to complete the full address)*

---

### 4. Configure Optional Settings

You may also configure the following options:

- **Phone Number** – Contact number for the calibrator
- **Click to Email Images** – Enables sending images and documents with orders
- **Enable Calibrator** – Allows the calibrator to be active and used for orders  
  - Disabling keeps the record but suspends usage
- **Default Delivery Instructions**  
  *(Example: “UPS Ground”, “FedEx Red”)*
- **Send Notes** – Sends notes entered in the Notes box with the order
- **Notes Box** – Additional information sent with calibration orders
- **Hide Pricing** – Prevents pricing from being shown
- **Default Reference Number** – Blanket PO number assigned to calibration orders
- **Expiration Date** – Date when the default PO expires

---

### 5. Finalize the Calibrator

1. Review all entered information.
2. Click **Create Supplier** to save.

Once created:
- The supplier will appear in the **Supplier List**
- The **Create Supplier** button changes to **Update Supplier**
- The calibrator is now available for calibration orders

---

## Managing an Existing Calibrator

- Use **Update Supplier** to edit contact details, notes, or PO information
- Disable the calibrator if it should no longer be used, without deleting it
