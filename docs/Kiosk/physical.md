# Physical Inventory

Physical Inventory allows **administrators to manually correct inventory counts** when quantities are inaccurate or items must be physically added or removed from the system. This process is intended for **inventory cleanup and reconciliation**, not for restocking or issuing tools.

**Also known as:** *Physicals*, *Manual Inventory Adjustment*

---

## When to Use Physical Inventory

Physical Inventory is typically used:

- To correct incorrect item counts
- To clean up inventory discrepancies
- Prior to running a **Purchase Order (PO)** to ensure accurate ordering
- Anytime inventory errors are discovered due to machine issues or training needs

> **Important:** Physical Inventory changes **do not create tracking history**. Use this method strictly for inventory corrections—not for issuing or returning tools.

---

## Access Requirements

- Only users with **administrator rights** can perform Physical Inventory adjustments.

---

## How to Perform a Physical Inventory

### 1. Log In

1. Sign in using your assigned credentials.
2. Enter your information and click **Submit**.

After logging in, you will see the **front page of your site**, displaying items at your station.

---

### 2. Open Physical Inventory

1. From the site front page, click the **Physical** link

This opens the Physical Inventory list.

---

### 3. Find an Item

Items can be located using:

- **Cabinet**
- **Drawer**
- **Search box**
- Scrolling through the item list
- Clicking directly on an item line


---

### 4. Select an Item

- Click **anywhere on the item’s row** to select it  
- There is **no separate “Select” link**—the entire row is clickable

---

### 5. Adjust the Quantity

1. A popup window appears with the selected item.
2. The **original quantity** is shown in the *New Quantity* field in light gray.
3. Enter the **correct quantity** in the quantity field.
4. Click **OK** to save the new quantity.

---

### 6. Return to Home

- Use the **Back** link at the top of the page to return to the home screen after completing the adjustment.

---

## Key Notes and Warnings

- Physical Inventory updates inventory counts **without tracking history**.
- Do **not** use Physical Inventory to:
  - Restock items
  - Remove tooling for usage
- Use the **Issue / Checkout process** for everyday item usage instead.
