# PO Receipt Reversal

PO Receipt Reversal allows users to **reverse a previously received purchase order line** so the transaction can be corrected and reprocessed. This is commonly used when an incorrect quantity or item was received.

**Also known as:** *Restock Reversal*, *Reverse PO Receipt*

---

## How to Reverse a PO Receipt

### 1. Open Restock

1. From the frontend software, click the **Restock** link.
2. Open the **PO List**.

---

### 2. Find the Purchase Order

1. Click **View PO Receipts**.
2. Enter the **Purchase Order number** you want to look up.
3. Click **Go**.

---

### 3. Reverse the Transaction

1. Locate the receipt line you want to reverse  
2. Click the **Reverse** button next to the transaction.

---

### 4. Confirm Reversal

1. A warning screen appears indicating you are about to reverse a purchase order line.
2. Click **OK** to continue.

---

### 5. Verify Reversal

- A confirmation message appears stating **"Reversed successfully"**.
- Once reversed:
  - The transaction is removed from receipts
  - The purchase order becomes available again for editing

If no purchase orders appear immediately, this is expected until a transaction has been reversed.

---

### 6. Retrieve the Purchase Order

1. Return to the PO list.
2. Click **Select** on the purchase order.
3. The purchase order opens, allowing you to:
   - Re-enter the correct quantity
   - Continue processing the receipt accurately

---

## Important Notes

- Reversing a receipt restores the purchase order so it can be worked on again.
- Always verify the correct transaction before clicking **Reverse**.
- Use this process only when a receipt was entered incorrectly.
