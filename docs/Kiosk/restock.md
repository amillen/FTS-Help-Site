# Restock Purchase Orders

Restocking purchase orders allows users to **receive ordered inventory into the system**, update quantity on hand, and track purchase orders until they are fully received. Using the **Restock** module ensures accurate inventory counts and proper purchase order tracking.

**Also known as:** *Restock Inventory*, *Receive Purchase Orders*, *PO Receiving*

---

## How to Restock a Purchase Order

### 1. Log In

1. Sign in using the credentials assigned to you.
2. Enter your information and click **Submit**.

After logging in, you will see the **front page of your site**, where items at your station are displayed.

---

### 2. Open the Restock Module
1. Click the **Restock** link.
2. The **Open Purchase Order List** appears.
3. Locate the correct purchase order or search by packing slip number.
4. Click **Select** to open the PO Receiving screen or **Go** if using the packing slip number.

---

### 3. Verify Quantity On Hand (Required)

Before receiving items:

- Verify the **Quantity On Hand** matches the physical count in the drawer.
- This creates a reliable starting point and helps identify discrepancies.
- If quantities do not match and research confirms the PO is correct, perform a **Physical Adjustment**.

---

### 4. Receive Items on the Purchase Order

For each line item:

1. Review:
   - Quantity ordered
   - Quantity already received
   - Quantity remaining
2. Enter the amount in the **Quantity to Receive** field.
3. Click **Receive** to move to the next item.

Notes:

- Partial shipments are allowed.
- If nothing is received, leave the field blank.
- The purchase order remains open until all items are fully received.

---

### 5. Cabinet and Drawer Guidance

During receiving:

- The item’s **location** is displayed at the top of the screen.
- A **bird’s-eye view** of the drawer is shown.
- A **cabinet view** appears in the lower-left corner.

This ensures items are stocked in the correct location.

---

## Physical Adjustments (If Needed)

If the physical count does not match the system count:

1. Click the blue **Quantity On Hand** hyperlink.
2. Enter the correct amount in **Physical Quantity BEFORE Receive**.
3. Enter the **Quantity to Receive**.
4. Verify the calculated **Physical Quantity AFTER Receive**.
5. Click **OK** to save.

The corrected quantity will update automatically on the receiving screen.

---

## Completing a Purchase Order

- After all line items are processed (fully or partially), the completion screen appears.
- Ensure **all drawers are closed**.
- Click **OK** to return to the Open Purchase Order list.

When all items are fully received, the purchase order is automatically removed from the list.

---

## Important Notes

- **Do not use the Physical method to restock purchase orders.**
  - Physical is for inventory corrections only.
  - Using it for restocking will prevent PO tracking and clearing.
- Monitor how long purchase orders remain open.
- Weekly open PO reports are recommended to prevent short accounts.
