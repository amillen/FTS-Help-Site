# Vending Machine Kiosk Troubleshooting

**Vending machine kiosk troubleshooting** is the process of **identifying and fixing problems in self‑service vending machines**, especially modern vending kiosks that use touchscreens and networked software.

---

## Common Vending Machine Kiosk Problems

### 1. Hardware Issues
- **Machine not powering on**
- **Product not dispensing**
- **Drawer not opening**
- **Touchscreen not responding or misaligned**
- **Screen black**
- **Card reader / RFID reader / QR not working**


### 2. Software Issues
- **Frozen screen or System wont logging in**


### 3. Network & Connectivity Issues
- No internet or weak signal
- Wired connection or Wi‑Fi failure

---

## Basic Vending Machine Kiosk Troubleshooting Steps

1. **Check power supply**
   - Ensure machine is plugged in

2. **Restart the machine**
   - Power cycle to reset software and cabinet

3. **Inspect the locks and drawers**
   - Check make sure all drawer are closed and locked
  
4. **Touchscreen not Responding**
   - Power cycle the kiosk by unplugging power for 10 seconds

5. **Screen Black**
   - Check HDMI and Power cables are plugged in

6. **Card reader not Responding**
   - Unplugging card reader for 10 seconds

7. **Unable to login to the kiosk**
   - Logoff kiosk completely and sign back in with email and password for **ftsware.com**

8. **Check connectivity**
   - Verify Wi‑Fi or Ethernet is working
