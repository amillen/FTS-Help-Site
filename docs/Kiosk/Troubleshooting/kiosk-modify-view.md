# Modifying Your Local Kiosk View

Modifying your local kiosk view allows supervisors to **add, remove, or change which cabinets are visible and usable at a specific kiosk location**. This is required whenever cabinets are added or moved, because kiosks only retain their **last saved configuration**.

**Permissions required:** *Able to Configure Kiosk*

---

## When to Modify the Kiosk View

You should modify the kiosk view when:

- A **new cabinet** is added
- A cabinet is **moved to a new location**
- You want to **change which cabinets are vended or viewable**
- Kiosk display, reporting, or timeout behavior needs adjustment

> **Note:** Changes apply **only to the kiosk you are currently logged into**. Each kiosk must be configured separately.

---

## How to Modify Your Local Kiosk View

### 1. Log In

1. Sign in using the **kiosk credentials** assigned by an administrator.
2. Click **Submit**.
3. If prompted, enter your **PIN**.

After logging in, you will see the **front page of your kiosk site items**.

---

### 2. Open Kiosk Settings

1. Click the **gear icon** in the top-right corner of the kiosk screen.

If you do not see the gear icon, you do not have permission to configure the kiosk. Contact your administrator.

---

### 3. Select Change Cabinets

From the gear menu, choose one of the following:

- **Change Cabinets** – Add, remove, or modify cabinets and kiosk options
- **Completely Logoff** – Log out to switch to a different cloud site

Select **Change Cabinets** to continue.

---

## Cabinet List Page

The **Cabinet List Page** is where you configure how the current kiosk behaves.  
Entering this page **resets all previous kiosk settings**, so the kiosk must be fully reconfigured to the desired setup.

---

## Configure Kiosk Options

From the Cabinet List Page, you can:

- Choose which **cabinets are vended or viewable**
- Configure **reporting fields**
- Modify the **item display view**
- Set the **hours allowed to adjust items back**
- Set the **Auto Logout Time**
- Enable or disable **Birdseye View**
- Enable **Checker Mode (Tool Crib Mode)**
- Enable **Virtual Keyboard (This is for Touchscreen Kiosk)**

Click **Select Cabinets** when finished to save and apply your changes.

---

## Verify Your Changes

Always verify that your changes were applied correctly:

- Try viewing or checking out an item from a newly added cabinet
- Confirm time limits and kiosk behavior function as expected
- Ensure the correct cabinets and items are visible

> **Best Practice:** Never leave the kiosk without confirming the changes work as intended.
