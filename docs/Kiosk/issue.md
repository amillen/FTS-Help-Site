# Issue Items

Issuing items allows users to **check out inventory for everyday use** while automatically updating inventory counts. The process follows a shopping-cart-style checkout flow and guides users to the correct cabinet and drawer location.

**Also known as:** *Issue Out Items*, *Checkout Inventory*

---

## How to Issue Items

### 1. Log In

1. Sign in using the credentials assigned to you.
2. Enter your information and click **Submit**.

After logging in, you will see the **front page of your site**, where all items available at your station are displayed.

---

### 2. Find Your Item(s)

You can search for items in **three ways**:

- **Search Box**
- **Pages**
- **[Categories](xref:categories)**

---

### 3. Add Items to Cart

1. Click **Add to Cart** for the desired item.
2. Once added:
   - The item appears in your cart with the selected quantity
   - A message appears stating *“This item is in your cart”*

To remove an item:
- Click **Remove** under the item, or
- Hover over the item image in the cart and remove it there.

---

### 4. Checkout

Before checkout:

- Complete any **required fields**
- Verify the correct items are selected
- Adjust quantities using **(+)** or **(-)**

Click **Checkout** to proceed.

---

## Cabinet and Drawer Access

- The system directs you to the correct cabinet
- The appropriate drawer unlocks automatically
- Once the item is removed and the drawer is closed, the cabinet locks again

### Drawer Location Example

- **Cabinet:** 01
- **Drawer:** 02
- **Column:** B
- **Row:** 01

---

## Undo a Checkout (Important)

If the wrong item is selected:

- Click **Undo** **before closing the drawer**
- This cancels the transaction and prevents inventory count changes
