# Printing Bin Labels

Printing bin labels allows users to **create and label cabinet bins** for clear identification and organization. Labels can be generated directly from the Cloud and printed to a compatible printer for accurate alignment.

---

## How to Print Bin Labels

### 1. Open Your Dashboard

1. Sign in to the Cloud.
2. Navigate to your **Site Dashboard**.

From the dashboard, you can manage cabinets and generate bin labels.

---

### 2. Open the Cabinets Module

1. Click on the **Cabinets** module.


---

### 3. Access Print Labels

1. From the **Cabinet List**, click the **Gear icon**.
2. Select **Print Labels**.

---

### 4. Choose Label Settings

Before printing:

- Select the desired **Label Format**
- Configure label placement:
  - **Top**
  - **Bottom**

> Printing directly to a printer is recommended.  
> Printing to PDF may cause alignment issues when labels are printed.

---

### 5.Select What Labels to Print
- You have the option to print all labels or a selection of labels by **Deselect/Select ALL**

  - Print a drawer of labels. You can use the drop down to select one or more drawers
  - Print specific labels by unchecking locations you don't want to print

---

### 6. Print Bin Labels

1. Confirm your label settings.
2. Print directly to the selected printer.

Your bin labels will be generated and ready for placement on cabinet bins.

---

## FAQ

### Can you customize bin labels?
You can only change the location placement. You can print the locaiton on top of the barcode or the bottom of the barcode.

### Can you print item labels?
No you can only print location labels.
