# Assign Item to a Location through the Item module

Assigning an item to a location allows users to **link inventory items to specific cabinets and bins**, set minimum and maximum quantities, and prepare items for ordering and checkout workflows.

**Also known as:** *Item Location Setup*, *Assign Item to Bin*

---

## How to Assign an Item to a Location

### 1. Open the Items Module

1. From your **Dashboard**, navigate to your **Site Dashboard**.
2. Click on the **Items** module.
3. The **Item List** will be displayed.

---

### 2. Select an Item

1. In the **Item List**, click on the item you want to assign to a location.
2. The item details screen will open.

---

### 3. Open the Location(s) Tab

1. At the top of the item screen, click the **Location(s)** tab.
2. Click the hyperlinked **“here”** option to add a new location.

---

### 4. Select a Cabinet and Bin

1. Choose the appropriate **cabinet location** from the list.
2. Click **Select** to confirm the cabinet.

#### Bin Selection

- If **open bins** are available, they will be listed.  
  - Click **Select** next to the desired bin.
- If **no bins are available**, click **“here”** to create a new bin in an open location.

---

### 5. Set Minimum and Maximum Levels

1. Enter the **Min** and **Max** quantities for the item at this location.
2. Click **Apply** to save the settings.

---

### 6. Confirm the Location Assignment

After applying:

- The item will appear with:
  - Assigned location
  - Cabinet and bin information
  - Minimum and maximum quantities

---

## Next Steps

- If inventory is below minimum levels, click the **shopping basket** icon.
- This will take you directly to the **Purchase Order module** to place an order.
